About Us

Lara's Cafe, founded in 2023 in JSS Science and Technology University, Mysore, India, epitomizes excellence with six devoted employees. It champions quality and service, crafting dishes with locally sourced ingredients, prepared by hand with love. Trusted by numerous patrons, Lara's Cafe celebrates India's culinary heritage.
